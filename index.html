About VIIT Barwani

Premier Technical Institute Since 2001

Vivekanand Institute of Information Technology (VIIT) is a distinguished technical institute located in Barwani, Madhya Pradesh. Affiliated with Makhanlal Chaturvedi National University and AICTE approved, we're dedicated to nurturing innovative minds through quality education in information technology and allied fields.

Our mission is to become a premier center for technical excellence, contributing significantly to the nation's knowledge economy through cutting-edge education and industry collaboration. We emphasize innovation, practical learning, and ethical values to develop well-rounded IT professionals.
